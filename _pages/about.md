I am **Liuzhenghao Lv** (吕刘正浩), a Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science at Peking University. I am fortunate to be advised by [Prof. Yonghong Tian](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=fn6hJx0AAAAJ&hl=zh-CN) and [Prof. Li Yuan](https://yuanli2333.github.io/).

My research lies at the intersection of **large language models, scientific agents, and AI for Science**. I am particularly interested in building foundation models and agentic systems that can understand, generate, and reason over scientific objects such as molecules and proteins.

[[Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=lFUR8mQAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN)] [[DBLP](https://dblp.org/pid/365/7605)] [[GitHub](https://github.com/Lyu6PosHao)] [[Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/GreatCaptainNemo)]

Research Interests
-----
- **AI for Science**: molecular/protein foundation models, scientific representation learning, and scientific reasoning.
- **LLM Agents**: tool-augmented agents, workflow automation, and agentic scientific discovery.
- **Reliable Scientific LLMs**: hallucination evaluation, numeric understanding, and alignment for scientific tasks.
